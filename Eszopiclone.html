Warnings & Precautions for Eszopiclone

Eszopiclone comes with some warnings, and people must be aware of them. Below are some of them:

Inconsistent behavior: Eszopiclone may cause some inconsistency in the people' behavior. People may start doing activities when they are not completely awake. It may include getting ready to eat food, driving, making calls, or indulging in sexual activities. People need to get in touch with a physician immediately.

Suicidal thoughts: If people begin to feel like ending their lives or think of harming themselves, they must connect to their health care professionals at the earliest. 

Liquor warning: We advise people to stay away from liquor or other alcoholic drinks as it may raise the risk of side effects. They may experience excessive drowsiness or sleepiness if they consume alcohol. We advise people not to consume alcohol if they had liquor in the evening.

Allergy warning: There are circumstances when people may face extreme allergic reactions after taking Eszopiclone tablets. The symptoms under allergy may include tongue swelling, throat swelling, breathing difficulty, vomiting, or nausea. If people get any allergic reaction, call the health care professionals without any delay. If people had an allergic reaction, they should refrain from taking the drug as taking it again may turn out to be fatal. 

For people with mental health issues: If the patient had a history of depression, taking medicine may surge the risk of suicidal thoughts. In such a scenario, people should take a lower dose of Eszopiclone. 

For pregnant women: There are no detailed studies regarding pregnant women and the risk that the drug may pose to the human fetus. Meanwhile, when the research was conducted on animals, it negatively affected the fetus when the mother took the drug. But, animal studies do not always highlight the way humans would react to the drug. It is better to avoid taking the drug in case if women patients have conceived for better safety.

People may also face some serious side effects when they take Eszopiclone tablets. It is important to speak to a physician if people face serious side effects. Given below are some of the serious side effects and their symptoms:
